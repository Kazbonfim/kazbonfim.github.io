Sobre mim


Olá, meu nome é Lucas, tenho 24 anos e sou apaixonado por tecnologia. Desde criança, quando ganhei um MegaDrive foi amor á primeira vista, de lá pra cá eu sempre brinquei de colocar meus desenhos nas telas dos computadores, criando pequenos jogos (principalmente de RPGs, que sou fã confesso).

About me


Hello, my name is Lucas, I'm 24 years old and I'm passionate about technology. Since I was a child, when I got a SEGA Genesis it was love at first sight, since then I've always played with putting my drawings on computer screens, creating small games (mainly RPGs, which I'm a confessed fan).
When I discovered that creating websites was not as complex as developing games, I decided to put my ease in learning certain patterns and my creative skills to use. Today I'm studying autonomously, but I intend to take a technical course in the area, to develop such knowledge professionally - 30% HTML, 30% CSS and 40% Javascript!
